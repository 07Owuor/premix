About Premix Summit

On behalf of Novo Nordisk, it is with great pleasure that we welcome you to Nairobi for the “1st Middle Africa Premix Summit” which will be held in on Saturday the 8th of October. The meeting will take place at the Radisson Blu Hotel in Nairobi’s upper Hill Area and begins at 8:30 a.m. We are delighted that you are interested in attending this unique event and hope that you find the meeting stimulating and enjoyable. This Summit has brought together diabetes specialists from across the globe. We will discuss individualized management of type 2 diabetes using achievable goals and simplified treatment regimens. In addition, we will discuss the latest data on the different treatment options and guidelines for insulin initiation and intensification. You will have the opportunity to submit questions and engage in discussion with the faculty during the panel discussions and interactive sessions. We look forward to your participation throughout the Summit. We hope you enjoy what will surely be a successful and informative meeting.
